About GameTop


GameTop was made for gamers so they could get better at gaming. GameTop is also for an app for you to track your skill on the go. Even though that GameTop is a computer you can link your computer to your phone using Bluetooth or your account.



How this works


1. Connect your GameTop monitor to your computer and keyboard

2. Link your phone via Bluetooth or your account email to the monitor, download the app in the App store or Google Play.

3. Create an account!
